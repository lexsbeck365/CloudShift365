How It Works

Our Migration Process

A structured, proven methodology that keeps every migration on track — from first conversation to final cutover.

1

Discovery & Assessment

We audit your current environment, document dependencies, identify blockers, and map the ideal target architecture in Azure / Microsoft 365.

2

Migration Plan & Proposal

You receive a detailed project plan with timeline, scope, resource requirements, risk register, and a fixed-price proposal before any work begins.

3

Pilot Migration

We migrate a small subset of users or workloads first — validating the process, testing coexistence, and adjusting the plan based on real results.

4

Full Cutover

Batch-by-batch migration executes on a schedule approved by your team, with rollback procedures ready and real-time status updates throughout.

5

Stabilization & Handoff

Post-migration support, documentation delivery, and knowledge transfer to your IT staff so your team owns and understands the new environment.

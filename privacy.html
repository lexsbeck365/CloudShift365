Who We Are

CloudShift365 LLC ("CloudShift365", "we", "us", or "our") operates the website cloudshift365.com. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you visit our site or contact us.

Information We Collect

We collect information you voluntarily provide through our contact form:

  • First and last name
  • Business email address
  • Phone number (optional)
  • Migration type and project description

We do not use tracking cookies, analytics scripts, or advertising pixels on this website.

How We Use Your Information

Information submitted through our contact form is used solely to respond to your inquiry and discuss potential migration services. We do not sell, rent, or share your information with third parties for marketing purposes.

Contact Form — Third-Party Processor

Our contact form is powered by Formspree. When you submit the form, your data is transmitted to and processed by Formspree in accordance with their privacy policy. We receive the submitted data via email.

Google Fonts

This website loads fonts from Google Fonts. Google may collect your IP address and browser information when fonts are requested. No personal data you submit to us is shared with Google.

Data Retention

We retain contact inquiry data only as long as necessary to respond to and fulfill the inquiry, or as required by applicable law.

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, or delete your personal data. To exercise any of these rights, contact us at info@cloudshift365.com.

Children's Privacy

This website is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision.
